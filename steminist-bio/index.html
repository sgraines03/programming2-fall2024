Susan Greenfield

Susan Greenfield is a leading British neuroscientist, author, and CEO of NeuroBio, a company focused on brain research. She is known for her work on neurological diseases and the effects of technology on the brain. Greenfield has written several popular books, such as Mind Change and The Human Brain. Greenfield has been reconized for many honors, including the Michael Faraday Award and being named CBE (Commander of the Order of the British Empire).
